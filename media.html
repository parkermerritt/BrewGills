REEL TIME CINEMAS: COMING SOON

In creating the project I learned just how difficult each step of the process was. From cover design, to marketing, to actually writing and editing, each step is respectively demanding. I muscled my way through the process half-assedly and ended up creating a botched, half-finished, project. It’s not that I hate the stories within the collection, but, I hate that I rushed myself. The cover is atrocious, the content isn’t properly edited, and it just screams, “I hurried to create this just to have something created!” I don’t want that to be my voice. I wanted to gain insight, I did that, and now i’m moving forward.
