Official BrewGills Rules

  1. An official BG shotgun doesn’t count if the remaining (settled) beer in the bottle exceeds 2 fluid ounces.
  2. The timing of all BG shotguns are to start and end when the bottle is parallel to the ground.
  3. We will only allow brews with volumes of 12 fluid ounces to count for our leaderboards, to ensure consistency across all BrewGillers.

Tips from BrewGills HQ

  1. We’ve found that the most effective method of recording BG shotgun times is by recording a video of the shotgunner downing the brew, and then using a stopwatch to verify the approximate shotgun time.
  2. We prefer to position the Hook to the right or left sides of the bottle opening, rather than top or bottom. This has proven to be more ergonomic and beer-efficient in the duration of our shotgun tests.
